Oatmeal

Descriptiom

Easy oatmeal breakfast that keeps one full for hours. Serve cold or warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quick cooking oats
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 banana, mashed
  • 3 tablespoons flax seed meal
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter

Steps

  1. Whisk oats, milk, yogurt, banana, flax seed meal, and peanut butter together in a bowl.
  2. Refrigerate until set, at least 15 minutes.
